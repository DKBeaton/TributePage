Hayao Miyazaki

Animator, filmmaker, screenwriter, author, and mangaka.

Hayao Miyazaki (宮崎 駿) is a co-founder of Studio Ghibli, a film and animation studio, he has attained international acclaim as a masterful storyteller and as a maker of animated feature films, and is widely regarded as one of the most accomplished filmmakers in the history of animation.

“Always believe in yourself. Do this and no matter where you are, you will have nothing to fear.”
— Hayao Miyazaki
